12TH GRADE PROJECT

In the last year of high school, in order to validate our project of option Information and Digital System, each of us had to find a technical solution in order to make a classroom more modern and ecological. For that we had to dispose different sensors and microcontrollers, a breadboard connected to a Raspberry Pi by GPIO. First we had to choose the different devices to integrate to the home automation circuit of the room according to different functional and ecological criteria, then we had to design a miniature model of the home automation circuit on the breadboard with the miniature devices (LEDs for the bulbs, a miniature motor for the shutters, a fan box for the CMV, . ...and other similiar things), once the circuit is wired, we had to use different Python libraries to create a graphical interface (with tkinter) that can drive the home automation system (pigpio and ads2015) and display the current status of the different devices as well as different information related to the room, such as temperature and humidity as well as the brightness of the room
